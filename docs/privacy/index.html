Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Victor Odukoya ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our apps and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our apps, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when signing in with Google
  • User Content: Catalogs, word pairs, and other content you create
  • Support Requests: Information you provide when contacting support

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Gameplay statistics, feature usage, and app interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Crash Reports: Technical information to help us fix issues

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our apps and services
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Sync your data across devices (if you opt in)
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important updates about the app (not marketing)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to use cloud sync features, your data is stored securely on servers provided by Supabase (AWS infrastructure).

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Secure authentication practices
  • Regular security reviews

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and personalized ads

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

European Union (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Legal basis for processing: Legitimate interests, consent, and contract performance.

California (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use our apps. If you delete your account:

  • Your data is deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 2 years
  • Backup copies are deleted within 90 days

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision.
